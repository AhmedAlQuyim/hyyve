01 / Essence

Collective intelligence, made calm.

Hyyve helps mid-market businesses orchestrate AI agents and automation. The brand sits between two truths: the swarm — many small intelligences working together — and the hub — one calm, central place to direct them. Cool, considered surfaces. One warm signal of life.

Posture

Quietly confident.

We don't shout. We're the steady hand on the wheel of your operations.

Promise

Less busywork. More business.

Agents handle the repetition; your team handles the judgment.

Metaphor

Hive, not swarm.

Cells, structure, choreography — never chaotic, never frantic.

02 / Logo

Wordmark.

Set in Fraunces Light. The double-y is the signature — two stems mirrored to suggest a hexagonal cell, two agents in dialogue. The terminal dot is the only place the warm amber appears in the wordmark; it's the spark of intelligence.

hyyve.
Construction

Fraunces, weight 300, optical size 144. Letter-spacing -4%. The dot is set in the brand amber at the same x-height as the "e" terminal.

Clear space

Minimum padding equal to the cap-height of the "h" on all sides.

04 / Typography

Fraunces & Inter Tight.

Fraunces brings warmth and editorial poise to display work — its soft optical sizing reads more "letterpress" than "tech." Inter Tight handles UI and body for clean, dense legibility. JetBrains Mono provides technical credibility in metadata, labels, and code.

05 / Motif

The cell.

A hexagon is the only literal nod to the hive — used as a structural device, never decorative. It appears in the logo glyph, as a chart frame, and as a layout grid for case-study cards. We never draw bees, honeycomb textures, or honey drips.

08 / Voice

How we talk.

Plain English, mid-sentence confidence. We say "agent," not "AI-powered intelligent assistant solution." We never explain that AI is "the future."

— We say

"A calmer way to run AI."

"We ship the agents. You keep the team."

"Less busywork. More business."

— We don't

"Revolutionary AI-powered solutions for the future of enterprise."

"Unlock the power of next-generation intelligent automation."

"Synergize your workflows with our cutting-edge platform."
